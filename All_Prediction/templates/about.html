About

DiagnosAI is a remarkable ML-based application that enables users to predict their health status by inputting various parameters. With a user-friendly interface, it harnesses the power of machine learning to provide personalized health predictions. This innovative tool empowers individuals to take proactive measures towards their well-being. By entering relevant data, users can gain valuable insights into their health and potential risks, making it an invaluable asset for healthcare management.
